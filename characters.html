Meet the Heroes of the MCU

Iron Man

Tony Stark is a genius inventor and billionaire, known for his quick wit and iconic suit of armor.

Fun Fact: Tony built his first suit while being held captive!

Thor

The God of Thunder, Thor wields Mjolnir and defends both Asgard and Earth from danger.

Fun Fact: Thor is over 1,500 years old!

Captain America

Steve Rogers, a WWII hero, was enhanced by the Super-Soldier Serum, becoming the symbol of hope for America.

Fun Fact: He was frozen in ice for nearly 70 years!
